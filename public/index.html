ABOUT US

Founded in November, Mind And Medicine Holidays LTD is a premier travel company specializing in wellness retreats and medical tourism. We offer curated experiences that combine healing, relaxation, and holistic well-being across the globe.

CORE SERVICES

Wellness Retreats — Yoga, fitness, meditation, mental health education, detox, personal training, and stress-relief programs in exotic locations.
Medical Tourism — Access to high-quality healthcare, cosmetic surgery, dental treatments, and alternative therapies.
Holistic Healing — Ayurveda, Traditional African Medicine, Traditional Chinese Medicine, energy healing, and spa therapies.
Adventure & Mindfulness — Nature-based retreats combining adventure travel with mindful practices.
Corporate Wellness — Tailored retreats for teams focusing on burnout recovery and mental resilience.
